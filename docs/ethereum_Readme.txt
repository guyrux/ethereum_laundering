Description:

Dataset contains KPIs aggregated at an account level generated on all account historic data. A total of 4681 ethereum accounts are present; with the 'Flag' column denoting
whether the respective account has been flagged by the Ethereum community (https://etherscamdb.info/) for carrying out illicit acitivity or not;
(1 = 'illicit accounts', 0=normal accounts)

Account #;
2179 accounts flagged as 'Illicit accounts' 
2502 accounts flagged as 'Normal accounts'
47 KPIs per account
